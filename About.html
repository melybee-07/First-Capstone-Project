Creative Commons Global Summit 2023

A community of generative art creation using machine learning techniques brings together artists, developers, and researchers who are passionate about exploring the intersection of art and technology. Using machine learning algorithms and neural networks, these creators are able to generate unique and complex art forms that are impossible to create by hand. By leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, they are able to create dynamic and ever-changing pieces of art that challenge our traditional notions of what art can be. This community is constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible with machine learning, and through collaboration and experimentation, they are creating a new form of art that is both innovative and exciting.
